Find Us

Gatland Lane Play Area, off Gatland Lane or Elmstone Lane, Maidstone ME16 8BS

Helpful directions are provided underneath the map below from key locations.

Directions

Select the Directions button in the embedded map or View Larger Map.

For our regular attendees, the park is 3 minutes walk from the church - PLEASE DO NOT PARK AT THE HALL as another event is taking place.

Walking directions from the church are shown below.

Parking

There is NO parking at the park. Please park with respect for our neighbouring community. On street parking is limited but free. Please do not park in Elmstone Lane, this is a private road.

Facilities

There are NO Toilet Facilities at the park and none in the immediate vicinity.

Arriving by Train

The nearest train station is Maidstone West. We are located 2km walk from the station or 7 minutes by bus.

Arriving by Bus

The closest bus stop is Cherry Tree. This stop is served by routes 3 and 7.
