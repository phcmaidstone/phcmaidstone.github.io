Sunday Service in the Park

Join us for a special Sunday Service in the park.

Event Details

Our service will begin promptly at 2.30 PM and will start with a couple of Praise & Worship songs followed by a short message (around 15 minutes) delivered by our Pastor.

After our service, you are welcome to join us for a time of fellowship and chat.

Are services are welcoming, friendly and open to anyone. Come as you are, leave renewed!

Questions?

If you have any questions, please contact us in advance.

God willing, see you there!
