Our Pastor

Meet our Pastoral Team

Pastor Robert

Pastor Robert gave his life to Jesus in 2018 and served in the Wandsworth Church in numerous ministries until being sent to pioneer our Maidstone Church in April 2024.

Born and raised in Maidstone and the surrounding villages, he attended nearby Oakwood Park Grammar School before studying at the University of Greenwich in London where he has lived ever since.

As a young man, he was a regular ballboy for Maidstone United in the days when their stadium was on the London Road!

He is married to Folusho and together they have four children.
