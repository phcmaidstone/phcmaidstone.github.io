HOPE Live Concert

Saturday 27th September 17.30

We're Glad You're Here...

Thank you for attending our concert today. We are Potter's House Church Maidstone. We hope you will enjoy, or have enjoyed, the HOPE: Live Concert.

...but there's more!

Our church here in Maidstone has a simple mission: To Reach the Lost (connect with those who don't yet know Jesus), Make Disciples (help people understand how to apply the teachings of Jesus to their every day lives) and to Plant Churches (spread the same mission to other Towns and Cities).

We strongly believe we can help you to start & grow in a relationship with Jesus Christ which will transform and greatly enrich your life. Every performer here tonight has invited Jesus Christ into their heart and it is His love that empowers them to live the lives you see before you today.

Join us on this journey

We'd love to see you at one of our church services. We meet at Fant Community Hall every Sunday at 14:30. Please do come and join us at our next Sunday service. Everyone is welcome.
