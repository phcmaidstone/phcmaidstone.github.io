Forgetting Those Things Which Are Behind

The first sermon in our mini-series to roll over 2024 to 2025, preached on Sunday 29th December 2024

Summary

2024 is almost behind us and 2025 is around the corner! Perhaps 2024 has not gone to plan for you. Maybe sin has crept in. Christ can wipe the slate clean and make all things new.

Or perhaps you've had a great year! It's equally important not to let success go to your head and allow pride to creep in.

We need to leave 2024 behind and approach 2025 with a new mindset. This sermon, based on Philippians 3:13-14, is the first in a mini two-part series to rollover the New Year 2024 to 2025. The message challenges believers to let go of the past and press forward toward their God-given purpose. Paul reminds us not to be complacent on our walk with Christ and to put all things behind us ensuring we focus on the heavenly prize that awaits!
