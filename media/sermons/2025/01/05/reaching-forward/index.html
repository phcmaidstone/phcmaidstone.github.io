Reaching Forward

The second and final sermon in our mini-series to roll over 2024 to 2025, preached on Sunday 5th January 2025

Summary

The sermon "Reaching Forward" based on Philippians 3:13-14 challenges believers to reach forward with focus and determination toward God’s ultimate calling. It encourages a life of intentional faith, striving for the eternal prize of being united with Christ and fulfilling His purpose.

Last week, we looked at letting go of past failures, regrets, and successes that can hinder spiritual growth.

In the second Part, we lay down a challenge to Reach Forward with faith and to not loose sight of the prize of the upward calling - to make heaven our home.

Listen to the Sermon
