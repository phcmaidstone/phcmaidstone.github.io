Launch Date Confirmed!

Our first service will be held on Sunday 17th November 2024

New Beginnings

We're delighted to confirm we now have arrangements in place to begin our church services in the town of Maidstone. We can't wait to get started and to meet you!

Begining Sunday 17th November, we'll be meeting every Sunday at 2.30PM at the Fant Community Hall.

We warmly welcome people from all walks of life to our church. Whether you’re new to faith, exploring Christianity for the first time, or a returning to Jesus, you will always find a place here at Potter’s House.

As we grow, we strive to be a family church firmly rooted in the local community and we are passionate about helping others discover the fullness and abundance that life has to offer through faith in Jesus Christ.

Hopefully, our website will answer any questions you have. Here are some popular items to get you started:

  • You can get directions to our venue.
  • Discover what we believe.
  • Read our statement of faith.
  • Follow us on social media.
  • Meet our pastor.

If you still have questions before your first visit, we’d love to hear from you!

  • Ask Us a Question

Come and give us a try, you've nothing to lose and everything to gain. We're confident you'll find a warm welcome - and a church you can call home.

We'll see you on Sunday 17th November!
