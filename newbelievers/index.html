I Just Prayed!

Wow! Congratulations, you have just surrendered your life to Jesus!

Your sins are now forgiven!

Today is a day of celebration. You have chosen to accept Jesus into your life as your Lord and Saviour

The bible tells us when we invite Jesus into our hear and declare He is Lord in our lives, are sins are forgiven and we are saved.

”If you declare with your mouth “Jesus is Lord” and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved.”
Romans 10:9

Jesus loves you unconditionally and today you have taken your first step in beginning your relationship with Jesus.

As a church, we would love to help you in any way we can on your journey with Christ. Please complete your details below and a member of our team will be in touch with some resources to help you.
