When & Where

Fant Community Hall

Every Sunday at 2:30 PM

We meet every Sunday at the Fant Community Hall, located just off the Tonbridge Road and minutes from the heart of Maidstone. Our services will start at 2.30 PM and will be followed by ample time for fellowship afterwards with tea, coffee - and a good selection of biscuits and healthy alternatives of course!

About Us

We are a Bible-based, Spirit-filled, Non-denominational church passionate about spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our goal is to help people who want to live their lives in the way that Jesus teaches us and that is by: Loving God, Loving People and Serving Others.

How we will help you

There's something for everyone at Potter's House Maidstone, including:

Weekly Church Services

Our Sunday Services will inspire, energise, and bring you closer to Jesus.

Bible Study Classes

Join one of our groups to study the bible together and learn the Word of God from each other.

Discipleship

Supporting one another to individually pursue a relationship with Jesus Christ.
