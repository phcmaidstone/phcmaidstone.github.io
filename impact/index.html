Information for Visiting Impact Teams

We genuinely appreciate your support in helping us build God's Church in Maidstone!

Welcome

This page is designed to give you all the information you need during your time in Maidstone.

Parking

The best three Car Parks are Fremlins Walk [ME14 1PS] (760 spaces / £11.40 = 6 hours), Maidstone East Station [ME14 1RG] (444 spaces / £5.60 = day) and Wheeler Street Car Park [ME14 1TU] (65 Spaces / £1.14/hr, max 14 hours).

There is no free street parking within a 20 minute walk of the Town Centre.

Trains

There are hourly trains direct from London Victoria to Maidstone East Station. A day return is £20-25.

Outreach

Please note we are not permitted to hand out flyers or preach on Fremlins Walk, Royal Star Arcade or in The Mall as these are all private land/properties. These are indicated in Red on the map above.

Our key locations/bases are either A (WH Smith), B (Metor Bank) or C (Town Hall) on the green circles shown.

The best streets to evangelise are highlighted in Green. Brecncly Gardens is a nice spot to walk around and sit with people on a good day.
